# **App Name**: Elementa Living

## Core Features:

- Dynamic Product Catalog: Display furniture items with images, titles, pricing, and ratings across various categories like chairs, sofas, and lighting, sourced from a product data system.
- Advanced Filtering & Sorting: Allow users to filter products by type (e.g., chairs, sofas), and sort by criteria such as 'Latest Products,' 'Best Sellers,' and 'Featured Products' using pill-shaped tab navigation.
- Shopping Cart & Wishlist Management: Enable users to add products to a shopping cart, view item counts with a notification badge, and save items to a wishlist via product card icons.
- Responsive Layout & Navigation: Implement a clean, grid-based layout with a full-width header, top notification bar, clear navigation links, and full responsiveness across mobile and desktop devices.
- Interactive Product Image Curatorial Tool: A generative AI-powered tool that suggests and retrieves high-quality, category-specific placeholder images from Unsplash (e.g., 'bar stools', 'modern sofas') based on product descriptions, ensuring visual consistency and adherence to styling guidelines.
- Flash Sale Timer & Product Discounts: Display specific products within the grid with sale badges (e.g., '50% off') and incorporate a dynamic countdown timer for limited-time 'Flash Sale' offers.

## Style Guidelines:

- The primary color, reflecting a deep, natural elegance, is Deep Forest Green (#004F3B), used for key interactive elements like buttons and badges. An elegant accent color, Brushed Gold/Mustard (#C5A05B), is used for ratings, secondary elements, and subtle highlights. The background is a soft, spacious neutral: cream or pure white (#FAFAFA or #FFFFFF).
- For striking headlines and prominent text, the geometric sans-serif font 'Poppins' will be used, lending a contemporary and precise feel. For all body text, subtitles, and detailed product descriptions, the grotesque-style sans-serif font 'Inter' will be used, chosen for its neutral tone and excellent readability, establishing a clear visual hierarchy.
- All icons will be sourced from 'Lucide React', ensuring a cohesive, minimalist line-art style. Key icons include search, shopping cart with a notification badge, user profile, wishlist hearts, expand icons, and distinct truck, wallet, and headset icons for value propositions, maintaining a clean and modern aesthetic.
- A clean, spacious, and highly organized grid layout defines the structure for product displays and category sections. All UI elements, including cards, images, and buttons, will feature smooth, rounded corners (using `rounded-2xl` or `rounded-3xl`). Careful attention to padding and margins will ensure optimal readability, visual balance, and full responsiveness across all devices. A unique 'Flash Sale' card with a dark background is integrated into the product grid to highlight temporal promotions.
- Subtle and elegant micro-interactions, powered by Framer Motion, will enhance user engagement. These include smooth transitions for hover states on product cards and buttons, seamless content loading, and engaging reveal animations for interactive elements like the wishlist and expand icons when hovering over product cards.
